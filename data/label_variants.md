# Transparency Label Variants (reference)

> Verbatim text finalized in Milestone 2 / README. Draft wording below; the label MUST change
> by confidence band and the "uncertain" variant must not read as an accusation.

## High-confidence AI  (`p_ai ≥ 0.80`)
> 🤖 **Likely AI-generated.** Our analysis strongly suggests this text was produced with the
> help of an AI tool. This is an automated estimate, not a certainty — the creator can appeal.

## Uncertain  (`0.30 < p_ai < 0.80`)
> ❔ **Origin uncertain.** Our signals disagree or are inconclusive about whether this text was
> written by a person or an AI tool. Treat the result as undetermined.

## High-confidence human  (`p_ai ≤ 0.30`)
> ✍️ **Likely human-written.** Our analysis suggests this text was written by a person, with no
> strong signs of AI generation. This is an automated estimate, not a guarantee.
